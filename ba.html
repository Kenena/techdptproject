Procedure for making the vase

  • Make the frame of the vase using the cardborad any desired shape can be used.
  • Weave the palm fronds into a net like deisgn. This design will be atttached to our frame.
  • Using the gum or stapler attach the fronds to frame.
  • Finally insert your decorative flowers into the vase.
